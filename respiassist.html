RespiAssist Series – Non-Invasive Neonatal & Pediatric Ventilation System

RespiAssist Series

Non-Invasive Neonatal & Pediatric Ventilation System

Next-Generation NIV for NICU & PICU Settings

The RespiAssist Series by Kyron Healthcare is a next-generation Non-Invasive Ventilation (NIV) system designed for NICU & PICU settings. Compact, precise, and user-friendly, it provides advanced respiratory support for neonatal and pediatric patients through intelligent electronic air–oxygen blending, real-time monitoring, and multiple therapy modes.
