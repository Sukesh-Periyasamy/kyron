Medical Humidifier - KHC-1000

Medical Humidifier

KHC-1000 (other model – 3000)

Precision Humidification for Safer Respiratory Therapy

The KHC-1000 is a robust and reliable humidification system engineered for advanced respiratory support. Designed with three customizable temperature modes and an enhanced alarm system, it ensures safe and consistent humidification for both neonatal and adult patients.

KEY FEATURES

Advanced humidification technology for optimal patient care

Three-Level Temperature Control

Offers temperature setting modes (P1, P2, P3) to meet varied clinical needs

Clear Digital Display

3-digit, 7-segment LED display for easy temperature visibility and control

Accurate Temperature Monitoring

Temperature range of 10–70°C, with ±2°C accuracy in the 25–45°C range

Dual Alarm System

Audio and visual alarms for overheat protection and patient safety

Thermal Cut-Off Safety

Automatic shut-off at 95±6°C for added protection

Compact & Durable Design

Built for continuous hospital use with high-performance components
