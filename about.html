About Us

KYRON HEALTHCARE PVT. LTD.

At Kyron Healthcare, we are committed to transforming neonatal and critical care by making advanced medical technology accessible, affordable, and designed for real-world conditions. With a deep understanding of the challenges faced by Indian hospitals—especially in rural and semi-urban areas—we develop life-saving devices like Respiassist Bubble CPAP that are portable, cost-effective, and easy to use. Backed by clinical insight and a passion for impact, Kyron is more than a healthcare company—we are a movement to give every newborn a fair start in life.

Our Vision:
Revolutionizing neonatal and critical care with accessible, life-saving innovations.

Our Mission:
To design and deliver affordable, easy-to-use medical technologies tailored for low-resource settings. We aim to bridge healthcare gaps through indigenous innovation and empower care providers globally.
